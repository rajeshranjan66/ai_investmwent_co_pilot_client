(5) Annualized Rate of Return (CAGR) CAGR is calculated by the formula: $$ \text{CAGR} = \left(\frac{\text{Final Investment Value}}{\text{Initial Investment Amount}}\right)^{1/n} - 1 $$ Where (n) is the number of years. Since our period is 1 year, (n = 1). $$ \text{CAGR} = \left(\frac{2313.90}{2111.10}\right)^{1/1} - 1 $$ $$ \approx 0.096 \text{or} 9.6\% $$
